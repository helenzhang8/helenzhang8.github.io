FoodBabyNY Map


For VISA0110: Digital 2D Foundation, one of my projects was to create a map exclusively using Adobe Illustrator. I was inspired by one of my favorite food bloggers, @foodbabyny. I wanted to make a map highlighting some of the spots frequented by FoodBabyNY, in hopes of one day visiting them myself.
