For my first project using React, my objective was to make a list, incorporating state and an npm package. I browsed for some packages and saw a few fun ones that caught my eye.

I ended up making a website that displays a list of fruit. Click on each fruit to generate a random color and emoji- click until you find one that adequately captures your emotions towards that fruit.
