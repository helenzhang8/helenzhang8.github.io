Over the summer, I finally caved and purchased some polymer clay. I have always wanted to experiment with polymer clay to make cute things. I don't have as much time now, but I do have a lot of ideas once the semester is over.

  • I use Sculpey Premo clay and Sculpey Gloss to seal the clay.
  • The clay items are cooked in my toaster oven on parchment with an aluminum tent on top.
  • The temperature is usually around 250˚F, but I have received a lot of mixed results.
  • I use acrylic paint for some projects. However, I lean towards making details out of clay for a better final result.
